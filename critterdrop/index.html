Critter Drop

Save the queen!

Get it now for
iPhone/iPad

Fun, physics puzzler!

Remove every block and get the queen critter to the green safety block. While you're at it, save as many other critters as you can! Don't forget to collect the stars! You can't advance to the next chapter without them!

Care was taken to create puzzles that require different techniques to win. Some require skill while others only luck, some a fast finger while others a more patient approach.

Good luck and save those critters!
